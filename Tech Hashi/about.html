about me · designer & developer

I'm Hashir khan

Frontend developer and web designer with a passion for dark, premium aesthetics. I design in Figma and bring those designs to life with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Every project is an opportunity to create something beautiful and functional.

My signature style: dark blue and black backgrounds, white text for readability, and gold (#d4af37) as a contrast accent. I believe that dark themes, when done right, feel luxurious and are easier on the eyes. I pay attention to typography, spacing, and micro-interactions.

I started 5 years ago and have since completed 40+ projects for clients worldwide — from portfolio sites to interactive web apps. I work directly with you, no middlemen, ensuring your vision is understood and executed perfectly.
